The Constitution is not a suggestion.

A framework with boundaries. Written on purpose. Meant to be followed — not “reinterpreted” into whatever is convenient today.

Take Action

Key points

  • Limited government: power is delegated — not assumed.
  • Separation of powers: no branch gets to do the job of another.
  • Checks and balances: authority must answer to authority.
  • Rights are protected: some freedoms are beyond majority vote.
  • Rule of law: not rule by personality, bureaucracy, or “exceptions.”
Plain truth: If the Constitution can be ignored when it’s inconvenient, it doesn’t protect anyone when it matters.
