Principle

Truth

Plain speech, honest thinking, and moral clarity. No fog. No decoration. No evasive language standing in for conviction.

Truth Is Still A Standard

Angry Abe treats truth as something sturdier than opinion. Words matter. Definitions matter. Public life falls apart when plain speech is replaced with convenient half-truths and borrowed slogans.

This page is the temporary home for that principle. It gives the homepage rotator a real destination now, and it can hold future blog posts, product drops, and longer commentary later.

The standard stays simple: say what is true, then let the words stand on their own.
