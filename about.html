Principles Over Politics

Angry Abe exists to remind people that character, liberty, and responsibility still matter.

Founder Story

Angry Abe started with a simple idea: some truths are timeless.

In a world full of noise, slogans, and constant outrage, the principles that built the United States are often forgotten. Ideas like personal responsibility, individual liberty, and the importance of character.

Angry Abe was created to put those ideas back into everyday conversation. Not through speeches or arguments, but through simple statements that make people pause and think.

Each design is meant to be clear, direct, and rooted in principles that have stood the test of time.

Mission

To promote timeless American principles like character, liberty, accountability, and respect for the Constitution through clear and thoughtful messages.

Values

Character matters more than reputation.

What people do defines who they are.

Principles matter more than politics.

Ideas should stand on their own merit.

Freedom requires responsibility.

Liberty works only when individuals take ownership of their actions.

Truth should be simple.

The most powerful ideas are often the most direct.

Why These Messages

Many of the statements used in Angry Abe designs come from ideas that have existed for generations.

They are reminders that some principles are not trends. They are foundations.

  • The Constitution Was Not a Suggestion
  • Reputation Is Not Character
  • Actions Reveal What Words Conceal

These statements are not about winning arguments. They are about remembering what matters.
