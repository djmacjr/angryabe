Why Character Matters More Than Reputation

Published March 3, 2026

In a world obsessed with image, reputation is often mistaken for character. But they are not the same thing.

Reputation is what people say about you. Character is what you do when nobody is watching.

History is full of examples where reputation changed overnight, but character stood the test of time.
